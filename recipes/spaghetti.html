Spaghetti recipe

Description

A very simple, yet delicious spaghetti dish to make with just a few ingridients.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of regular speghetti noodles
  • 16 oz of ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and garlic pepper
  • 1 Can of Rao's pasta sauce
  • 2 tbsp of red wine

Steps

  1. Start boiling pasta noodles
  2. Heat up sauce pan and add olive oil
  3. Toss diced onion into the pan
  4. Stir and cook for about a minute
  5. Add minced garlic and season with salt and pepper
  6. Once onions have a brownish color, add ground beef and mix it all together
  7. Season with salt and garlic pepper
  8. Once meat is fully cooked, add Rao's pasta sauce. Stir around and let it simmer a bit
  9. Then add a little bit of red wine and stir some more.
  10. After a couple of minutes of letting it simmer, your speghetti is ready!

Spaghetti recipe

Description

A very simple, yet delicious spaghetti dish to make with just a few ingridients.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of regular speghetti noodles
  • 16 oz of ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • Salt and garlic pepper
  • 1 Can of Rao's pasta sauce
  • 2 tbsp of red wine

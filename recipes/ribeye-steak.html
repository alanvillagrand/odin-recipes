Ribeye Steak

Description

The best way to sear a steak!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Ribeye
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • Some fresh thyme or other herbs

Steps

  1. Take steak out of refrigerator at least 20 minutes before cooking
  2. completely dry steak with paper towel
  3. Season both sides of steak with salt and pepper
  4. On a very hot pan, add the olive oil
  5. After bout 30 seconds, place the steak on the pan
  6. For medium rare, cook steak for 4 minutes on both sides
  7. After both sides have been cooked, lower the heat and add in the butter and herbs
  8. While the butter melts, grab a spoon and scoop the butter over the steak. Keep doing this to cover the steak with the butter
  9. After that, place steak on a rack or cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes. Feel free to pour the butter from to pan onto the steak
  10. After resting, dig in and enjoy!

Mushroom Omelette

Description

A simple, easy to make omelette for a great start to your morning!

Ingredients

  • 3 whole eggs
  • 3 mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 2 tbsp of milk
  • Some shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. On a heated pan, add olive oil and throw in sliced mushrooms
  2. Cook unti moisture is released
  3. Throw in a tablespoon of butter and mix with the mushrooms
  4. After the butter is fully melted and mixed with the mushrooms, place them to the side on a plate
  5. On the same pan, add another tablespooon of butter and melt
  6. Whisk 3 eggs and milk together with some salt and pepper
  7. Pour eggs into the pan and let it sit for a minute
  8. Push sided of omelete inward to make sure all of the egg is cooked
  9. Add cheese and place the cooked mushrooms back on the omelette
  10. Fold omelette, and place on a plate
  11. Top with some chopped green onions or parsley. Enjoy!
